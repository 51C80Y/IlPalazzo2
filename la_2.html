La Due

"La Due", located adjacent to "La Rustica", offers its own unique experience with a beautifully laid out open room with a connecting bathroom. This then connects to a small room which we have converted into a shower room. Lots of light, high celings and a waterfall shower, it might be the best shower you have ever had!

Every room comes with a unique private bathroom, fully furnished, with towels and linen. The kitchenette in the rooms is equipped with all the necessities for small meals and includes a kettle, sink and a fridge. All rooms also are equipped with a wood burner for the colder winter months. High speed Wifi available.
