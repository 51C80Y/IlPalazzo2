Rustica

The hotel is located on the Poggetto "le Fornaci", just a few meters from the historic center of Sassetta, "Il Palazzo", a unit divided into 3 studios with private bathrooms and small kitchenettes, with sinks, refrigerators and kettles with a wonderful view of the Mediterranean scrub.

La Due

"La Due" offers its guests a double bed and a single bed, a kitchenette for preparing cold meals with kettle, sink and fridge. The peculiarity of room Due is the bathroom in organic building materials and the large shower. The room overlooks the old quarry and offers a wonderful view of the Mediterranean scrub.

Drago

"La Camera Drago" with private bathroom, small cooking area with kettle, sink, refrigerator and stove to warm up in the colder months. Ideal for a stay to discover Sassetta and its surroundings or for a visit at the Terme di Sassetta.
