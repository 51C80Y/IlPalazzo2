Rustica

"La Rustica" room offers its guests a combination of rustic and modern with a magnificent view over the Tuscan forest. The room features a small kitchenette with sink, fridge and kettle, while a large fully equipped comunal kitchen is available for all guest on the first floor. A private bathroom built,as per the rest of the room, using natural materials such as Hemp and Lime. - All rooms have access to a communal Kitchen and living room space.

La Due

"La Due" offers its guests a double bed and a single bed, a kitchenette for preparing cold meals with kettle, sink and fridge. The peculiarity of room Due is the bathroom in organic building materials and the large shower. The room overlooks the old quarry and offers a wonderful view of the Mediterranean woods. - All rooms have access to a communal Kitchen and living room space.

Drago

"La Camera Drago" with private bathroom, and a small kitchenette with kettle, sink, refrigerator. The room offers a wood burner, to heath in the colder months. -All rooms have access to a communal Kitchen and living room space.
